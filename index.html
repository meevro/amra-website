Investment Thesis

Why healthcare real estate

Medical office buildings represent one of the most compelling opportunities in commercial real estate, supported by fundamental demographic and economic forces.

01

Demographic Tailwinds

The U.S. senior population is projected to reach 85 million by 2050, driving sustained growth in healthcare demand and outpatient services utilization across the country.

02

Recession-Resistant Demand

Medical services are essential regardless of economic conditions. Healthcare tenants maintain stable occupancy through market cycles, backed by government and insurance reimbursement.

03

Stable, Predictable Cash Flows

Long-term leases with credit-rated healthcare tenants provide dependable income streams. Built-in rent escalations and low tenant turnover further enhance cash flow reliability.

04

Institutional Demand

Growing institutional appetite for medical office portfolios creates strong exit opportunities. The sector has attracted billions in capital from leading institutional investors in recent years.
